A mod that adds compatibility between [K2SO](https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Krastorio2-spaced-out) and [Age of Production](https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Age-of-Production)

Compatiblity is aimed to be Krastorio-centric, removing machines or tweaking recipes to fit with it, not the other way around.

List of changes:
- Advanced Assembling Machine is removed in favor of K2SO machine of the same name
- Biochemical Facility is removed
- Quantum assembler/lab/beacon removed to maintain K2SO lategame ballance
- AOP Greenhouse now acts as a direct upgrade to K2SO Greenhouse. Productivity tech now affects K2SO recipes instead of AOP; AOP greenhouse recipes removed
- Smeltery has its ore melting recipes output reduced to match K2SO ratios
- Scrubber has its recipes rebalanced to take/return K2SO Pollution filter and half the amount of other ingredient. Recipe times and ingredients/products count raised to match the 4x speed of the K2SO Air Purifier. Pollution absorbtion buffed to be about 25% more efficient than Air Purifier per Pollution Filter
- Arc Furnace has its productivity bonus reduced 50% -> 25% and added as an ingredient for K2SO Advanced Furnace
- Specialized Science Pack recipes changed to output research data instead, icons adjusted accordingly
